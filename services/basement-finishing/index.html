Basement Finishing FAQs

Basement finishing typically costs $30,000 to $75,000+ depending on size, features, and finishes. A basic 1,000 sq ft basement starts around $30-40K, while luxury finishes with bathrooms and wet bars can exceed $75K. We provide detailed estimates during your free consultation.

Most basement finishing projects take 6-10 weeks from start to completion. The timeline depends on the size of your basement, complexity of the design, and whether you're adding bathrooms or wet bars. We provide a detailed schedule during planning.

Yes, basement finishing requires building permits in West Jordan and throughout Salt Lake County. We handle all permit applications and inspections for you, ensuring your basement meets all building codes and safety requirements.

Yes! We can add code-compliant bedrooms with required egress windows for emergency exit. Bedrooms must have proper ceiling height, egress windows, closets, and adequate heating/cooling. We ensure all bedrooms meet Utah building codes.

We assess your basement for moisture issues before starting and address any waterproofing needs. This may include sump pumps, drainage systems, or vapor barriers. Proper moisture control is essential for a successful basement finish.
