Kitchen Remodeling FAQs

Kitchen remodels typically range from $20,000 to $60,000+ depending on size, materials, and scope. Minor updates start around $15,000, while full luxury renovations can exceed $75,000. We provide detailed estimates during your free consultation and work with various budgets.

Most kitchen remodels take 4-8 weeks from demolition to completion. The timeline depends on the scope of work, custom cabinet orders, and any structural changes. We provide a detailed schedule during planning and keep you updated throughout.

Kitchen access is limited during remodeling, but we can set up a temporary kitchen area with a microwave and mini-fridge. We work efficiently to minimize disruption and restore functionality as quickly as possible.
