Flooring Options We Install

Choose the perfect flooring for every room in your home

Hardwood Flooring

Timeless beauty and warmth. Solid or engineered hardwood in oak, maple, hickory, and more. Adds value and elegance to any home.

Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP)

100% waterproof with realistic wood look. Perfect for kitchens, bathrooms, and basements. Durable, affordable, and easy to maintain.

Tile Flooring

Porcelain and ceramic tile in any size or pattern. Ideal for bathrooms, kitchens, and entryways. Water-resistant and long-lasting.

Carpet Installation

Soft, comfortable, and warm. Perfect for bedrooms and living areas. Wide selection of colors, textures, and styles.

Laminate Flooring

Budget-friendly wood look with scratch resistance. Great for high-traffic areas. Easy installation and maintenance.

Engineered Hardwood

Real wood veneer over stable core. More moisture-resistant than solid hardwood. Perfect for basements and concrete slabs.

Best Flooring for Each Room

Expert recommendations for every space in your home

Kitchen

High-traffic area that needs durability and water resistance.

  • Best: Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP)
  • Also Great: Tile, Engineered Hardwood

Bathroom

Moisture-prone area requiring waterproof materials.

  • Best: Porcelain or Ceramic Tile
  • Also Great: Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP)

Living Room

Showcase area where beauty and comfort matter most.

  • Best: Hardwood Flooring
  • Also Great: LVP, Carpet

Bedroom

Comfort and warmth for a cozy retreat.

  • Best: Carpet
  • Also Great: Hardwood, Engineered Wood

Basement

Below-grade space needing moisture-resistant options.

  • Best: Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP)
  • Also Great: Engineered Wood, Carpet

Entryway

High-traffic area exposed to dirt and moisture.

  • Best: Tile or LVP
  • Also Great: Hardwood (with rugs)

Flooring Installation FAQs

Flooring costs vary by material and room size. Carpet: $3-8/sq ft installed. Luxury Vinyl Plank: $4-10/sq ft. Hardwood: $8-15/sq ft. Tile: $5-15/sq ft. We provide detailed estimates during your free consultation with no hidden fees.

Most flooring installations are completed in 1-3 days depending on room size and material. Carpet and LVP are typically fastest (1-2 days). Hardwood and tile may take 2-3 days including prep work and finishing.

For kitchens and bathrooms, we recommend waterproof options: Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP) is 100% waterproof and looks like real wood. Porcelain or ceramic tile is also excellent for moisture-prone areas. Both are durable, easy to clean, and long-lasting.

We recommend removing small items, but we can help move larger furniture as part of our service. We'll discuss furniture moving during your consultation and include it in your estimate if needed.

Maintenance varies by material. Hardwood: sweep regularly, use wood floor cleaner. LVP: sweep and damp mop. Tile: regular mopping with mild cleaner. Carpet: vacuum weekly, professional cleaning annually. We provide detailed care instructions for your specific flooring.
