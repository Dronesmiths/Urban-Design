Custom Cabinetry FAQs

Custom cabinets are built from scratch to your exact specifications‚Äîany size, style, or configuration. Semi-custom cabinets start with standard sizes that are modified to fit your space. Custom cabinetry offers unlimited design possibilities, perfect fit for any space, and superior quality, while semi-custom provides good quality at a lower price point.

Custom cabinet costs vary based on size, materials, finish, and complexity. Kitchen cabinets typically range from $500-$1,500+ per linear foot. Bathroom vanities start around $2,000-$5,000. Built-in shelving runs $1,500-$5,000+. We provide detailed estimates during your free consultation.

The timeline for custom cabinets is typically 6-10 weeks from design approval to installation. This includes design (1-2 weeks), fabrication (4-6 weeks), and installation (1-2 weeks). We provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

We offer a wide range of wood species including maple, oak, cherry, hickory, and walnut. Finish options include painted (any color), stained, glazed, or distressed. We'll show you samples during your consultation to help you choose the perfect combination for your space.
