Our Story

Built on Trust & Quality

Urban Design & Remodel began with a simple mission: to provide homeowners in Salt Lake County with a remodeling service that combines high-end design with accessibility and functionality. We recognized a need for contractors who truly understand how to make a home beautiful and usable for everyone.

We set out to raise the standard. From humble beginnings, we've grown into West Jordan's premier choice for bathroom and kitchen transformations. We specialize in ADA-compliant modifications because we believe independence shouldn't come at the cost of style. We understand the local architecture and the needs of our community because this is our home.

Our team is vetted, highly trained, and dedicated to your vision. When we step into your home, we treat it with the same respect we would our own, ensuring a clean workspace and a seamless remodeling experience.
